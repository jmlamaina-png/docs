A study session is the foundation of everything you do in LaMaina.ai. You bring the material — a textbook excerpt, lecture notes, an article, or any text — and the AI transforms it into a structured learning experience. Each session gives you an instant summary, a personalized quiz, and access to visual learning tools like slides, flow charts, and anatomy maps.

## Creating a Session

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Study page">
    Go to the `/study` page in your browser. This is your main workspace for starting new sessions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a title">
    Type a descriptive title for your session — for example, "Chapter 4: Cell Division" or "French Revolution Notes". A clear title makes it easy to find the session later in your history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your study material">
    Paste the text you want to study into the material field. You can use lecture notes, textbook passages, article excerpts, or any written content you want to learn from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Start Studying">
    Click the **Start Studying** button. The AI immediately begins processing your material and generating a summary. You'll be taken to your session view within seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Material Limits

<Note>
  Study sessions support a maximum of **10,000 words**. For the best quiz coverage and most accurate results, aim for **500–5,000 words**. Very short inputs may not generate enough questions, while inputs near the maximum may produce broader, less focused quizzes.
</Note>

## AI Summary

As soon as you create a session, LaMaina.ai generates a comprehensive summary of your material. This isn't a minimal abstract — the AI preserves important nuances, key examples, and the context that makes concepts meaningful. You can use the summary to get a quick orientation before diving into questions, or revisit it later to refresh your memory without re-reading the full source text.

## Session History

Every session you create is automatically saved to your history. Navigate to `/history` to see all your past sessions. For each session, the history page shows:

- **Title** — the name you gave the session
- **Date** — when the session was created
- **Word count** — the length of the original material
- **Summary preview** — the first few lines of the AI-generated summary

Click any session in the list to resume it. You'll return to the full session view where you can retake the quiz, review your previous answers, or generate new visual materials from the same content.
