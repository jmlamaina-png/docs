The quiz feature is how LaMaina.ai turns passive reading into active learning. After you create a study session, the AI builds a set of questions that covers every important concept in your material. You answer each question one at a time, and the AI evaluates your response — telling you whether you're correct, explaining what you got right or wrong, and reinforcing the concept with a clear explanation.

## Generating Questions

Once you've created a study session, LaMaina.ai automatically generates questions from your material. The AI analyzes your text and produces up to **50 questions** that span the key facts, concepts, relationships, and arguments in your text. The questions are calibrated to the depth and breadth of what you pasted — a dense technical passage will produce different questions than a narrative summary.

<Note>
  Question generation happens automatically when you open a new session. Once the questions are ready, click **Take Quiz** to begin.
</Note>

## Answering Questions

Questions are presented one at a time. For each question:

1. Read the question carefully.
2. Type your answer in the text box.
3. Click **Submit** to send your answer to the AI for evaluation.

After you receive feedback, click **Next Question** (if your answer was correct) or **Proceed** (if your answer was incorrect) to move on to the next question.

## AI Evaluation

Every answer you submit is evaluated individually by the AI. After each submission you receive:

- **Correct or incorrect** — a clear `true` or `false` verdict on your answer
- **Personalized feedback** — a response that addresses what you wrote specifically, not a generic reply
- **Explanation** — a full explanation of the correct answer, giving you the context to understand why it's right even if you answered incorrectly

You can have the AI feedback read aloud using the **text-to-speech (TTS) player** that appears with each response. This is helpful for auditory learners or when you're studying away from a screen.

## Score and Progress

After you answer all the questions in your session, LaMaina.ai displays your final score along with a full review of every question. You can see:

- Which questions you answered correctly and incorrectly
- The feedback and explanation for each answer
- Your overall percentage score for the session

Use the review to identify the concepts you need to revisit, then go back to your study material or generate a new question set to keep practicing.
