The Presentations feature converts your study material into a clean, visual slide deck in seconds. Instead of manually picking out key points and hunting for images, you paste your text and let the AI build a structured 5-slide presentation — complete with bullet points and an AI-generated illustration for each slide. It's a fast way to review material visually or share a concept overview with others.

## How to Generate a Presentation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Study page">
    Navigate to the `/study` page in your app. If you already have a session open, scroll down to the **Generate Slides** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a descriptive title">
    Type a title that describes your subject clearly — for example, **"Biology Chapter 5"** or **"The Water Cycle"**. The title gives the AI context for how to frame and structure the slides.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your study material">
    Paste the text you want to turn into slides. The AI reads the full content and decides how to divide it into five focused, logical sections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Slides">
    Click the **Create Slides** button. The AI begins generating slide content and images simultaneously. Your presentation will be ready in a few seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your presentation">
    Browse through your generated slides directly on the page. Each slide is displayed with its title, bullet points, and illustration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's in Each Slide

Every slide the AI generates includes:

- **Slide title** — a concise heading for the topic covered on that slide
- **Key bullet points** — 3 to 4 focused points that capture the most important ideas from that section of your material
- **AI-generated illustration** — a visual image that represents the topic of the slide

The AI distributes your material evenly across all five slides so that the presentation flows logically from start to finish.

## Image Generation

Slide illustrations are created using **AI image generation**. If your LaMaina.ai instance is configured with a provider that does not support image generation, the slides will still be generated with their titles and bullet points — but the image panels will be empty. Contact your platform administrator if you expect images but aren't seeing them.

<Tip>
  Presentations work best with well-structured material that has clear sections, topics, or concepts. If your source text has headings, numbered sections, or distinct paragraphs, the AI can divide it into slides more accurately and produce more relevant images.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Presentations are not saved to your session history. If you want to keep your slides, take a screenshot of each slide or copy the bullet point content before navigating away from the page.
</Note>
