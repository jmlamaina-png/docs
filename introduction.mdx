**LaMaina.ai** is an AI-powered study platform designed for students, educators, and lifelong learners who want to get more out of their study time. Paste in any text — a textbook chapter, lecture notes, a research article, or your own written notes — and LaMaina.ai instantly transforms it into a rich set of learning tools: summaries, quizzes, slide presentations, flow charts, and anatomy maps. Whether you're cramming for an exam or building a deep understanding of a subject over time, LaMaina.ai adapts to your material and your pace.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Paste Your Material">
    Open a new study session and paste in any text — up to 10,000 words. You can use a chapter from a textbook, your own handwritten notes typed up, or any other written content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get an Instant Summary">
    LaMaina.ai processes your material and generates a structured summary that highlights the core concepts, key terms, and important relationships in the text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer AI-Generated Questions">
    Click **Generate Questions** to create a quiz tailored to your material. Work through the questions at your own pace — the AI evaluates each answer and explains why it is correct or incorrect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Your History">
    Every session is saved to your history dashboard. Return at any time to review your summary, re-take the quiz, check your previous score, or continue a session you left unfinished.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Self-host or use the cloud

LaMaina.ai can be deployed on your own infrastructure, giving you full control over your data, your choice of LLM provider, and your storage configuration. The self-hosted version runs on **Node.js**, stores data in **MySQL** (or any MySQL-compatible database), and connects to any **OpenAI-compatible** language model endpoint. You can also use the hosted cloud version and get started immediately without any setup.
