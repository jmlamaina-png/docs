## Your four-step learning loop

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Material">
    Paste any text — notes, chapters, articles, or transcripts — into a new study session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Summarizes">
    LaMaina.ai instantly distills your material into a concise, structured summary that highlights the key concepts.
  </Step>
